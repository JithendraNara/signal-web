# Roadmap

## Purpose
This roadmap tracks iterative delivery and proves sustained engineering progress over time.

- Improve reliability, testing depth, and observability.
- Resolve core backlog issues tagged for v0.2.

- Harden deployment workflows and SLO-oriented monitoring.
